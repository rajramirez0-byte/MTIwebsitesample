Our History

On July 22, 2003, Attorney Bagnos B. Tactay founded Equity Motorcycles Inc., dedicated to providing quality Honda motorcycles and spare parts. A commitment that would set the foundation for something greater. Then came Motorline Trading Inc., founded by Mr. Angel C. Tactay on June 8, 2011. A company born from passion, committed to trading motorcycles and spare parts—expanding its reach across Nueva Vizcaya, Quirino, Isabela, and Cagayan.

What we Aim

At Motorline Trading Inc. , we aim to provide quality motorcycles, spare parts, and excellent customer service that improve the everyday journeys of our customers. We are committed to making motorcycle ownership more accessible through trusted brands, convenient financing options, and dependable after-sales support. Our goal is to continuously grow alongside the communities we serve by delivering innovation, integrity, and a customer-first experience in every branch nationwide.
